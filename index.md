# Privacy Policy for Wutci

Last updated: April 2026

## Data We Collect

Wutci collects anonymous usage analytics to improve 
the app experience. This includes which features are 
used and how users interact with content. This data 
is not linked to your personal identity.

We use Posthog (EU region) for analytics. 
Data is stored in the European Union.

## Account Data

If you create an account, we store your email address 
solely for authentication purposes. We do not share 
this with third parties.

## Data We Do Not Collect

- We do not collect your name
- We do not sell your data
- We do not use advertising tracking
- We do not share data with third parties
